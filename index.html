About me

I'm a Compliance & Patient Safety Specialist based in Maryland. I started out as a dentist, moved into healthcare compliance, and then earned an MS in Health Information Technology at UMBC — so I read a clinical chart, an audit finding, and a SQL query with the same eye.

Day to day I keep healthcare organizations audit-ready: I maintain trackers for incidents, documentation gaps and privacy concerns, review patient-safety events and near-misses through to closure, and keep BAA, consent and SOP records in order. The work I like best sits where compliance meets data — turning messy source files into Excel and Power BI trackers people actually use, like the CMS nursing-home analysis in my Projects.
